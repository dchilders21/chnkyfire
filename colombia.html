Colombia

DJI Mavic Pro  +   Premiere Pro

Revisited Colombia for the second time in order to capture not only the beautiful beaches near the Caribbean Sea but also some of the exotic landscapes in the interior of the country. Starting in Medellín we made our way to Guatapé where we climbed to the top of El Peñón de Guatapé. There were breathtaking views of the surrounding areas but flying the Mavic Pro allowed us to see El Peñón from a different perspective. From there we had a sunset flight in Santa Marta getting a good view of this charming town on the edge of the Caribbean. In the morning, we made the mandatory trip to Parque Tayrona where you can obviously see why people from all over the world want to come here. Walking about 2 hours through the jungle only to be let out at the beach sitting right next to snow capped mountains is a once in a lifetime experience. Last stop was Cartagena where we kayaked to La Isla de Tierra Bomba so we can get some shots of Cartagena from afar.
